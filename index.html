About

Pedakallepalli is a Village in Mopidevi Mandal in Krishna District of Andhra Pradesh State, India.




It belongs to Andhra region . It is located 20 KM towards west from District head quarters Machilipatnam.



8 KM from Mopidevi. 354 KM from State capital Hyderabad



Pedakallepalli Pin code is 521130 and postal head office is Pedakallepalli.



Mangalapuram ( 4 KM ) , Kokkiligadda ( 4 KM ) , Merakanapalli ( 4 KM ) , Venkatapuram ( 4 KM ) , Salempalem ( 5 KM ) are the nearby Villages to Pedakallepalli.


Pedakallepalli is surrounded by Koduru Mandal towards South , Challapalli Mandal towards west , Avanigadda Mandal towards west , Ghantasala Mandal towards North .




Machilipatnam , Repalle , Pedana , Gudivada are the near by Cities to Pedakallepalli.




This Place is in the border of the Krishna District and Guntur District. Guntur District Repalle is west towards this place .




Famous Temples: Sri Durga Nageswara Swamy Temple : This is an ancient temple.




This temple was built in A.C.1140. It was later renovated by Challapalli Raja, Sri Yarlagadda Kodanda Ramanna in 1782.




There is a Govt. School, SDNSDS High School, within this village.
